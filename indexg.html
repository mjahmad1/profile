Discover Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is a destination for everyone. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway, a cultural immersion, or an adventurous thrill, you will find something to suit your taste and budget in this coastal paradise. Here are some of the types of travelers who can enjoy Cox's Bazar with Cox's AuraRead More

Families

Cox's Bazar is a family-friendly destination, with plenty of activities and attractions for kids and adults alike. You can enjoy the sandy beaches, the water park, the amusement park, the aquarium, and the wildlife sanctuary. You can also take a boat ride to the nearby islands, where you can snorkel, fish, or picnic. Cox's Aura offers family rooms, babysitting services, and special packages for families.Read More

Foodies

Cox's Bazar is a food lover's dream, with a variety of cuisines and dishes to satisfy your cravings. You can sample the local delicacies, such as fried fish, crab curry, prawn malai, and biryani. You can also try the international options, such as Thai, Chinese, Indian, and Italian. Cox's Aura has a restaurant, a cafe, and a bar, where you can enjoy delicious meals, snacks, and drinks.Read More

Culture & History Aficionados

Cox's Bazar has a rich and diverse history and culture, dating back to ancient times. You can explore the heritage sites, such as the Buddhist temples, the 100-year-old mosque, the colonial buildings, and the fishermen's village. You can also learn about the local customs, traditions, and festivals, such as the Rash Mela, the kite festival, and the fishing competition. Cox's Aura can arrange guided tours, cultural shows, and workshops for you.Read More

Nature Lovers

Cox's Bazar is a nature lover's paradise, with stunning landscapes and diverse wildlife. You can admire the longest natural sea beach in the world, the lush green hills, the turquoise sea, and the colorful coral reefs. You can also spot the rare and endangered animals, such as the Irrawaddy dolphins, the olive ridley turtles, the saltwater crocodiles, and the migratory birds. Cox's Aura can organize nature walks, wildlife safaris, and eco-tours for youRead More

Solo Travelers

Cox's Bazar is a safe and friendly destination for solo travelers, with plenty of opportunities to meet new people and have fun. You can mingle with the locals, the other tourists, and the Cox's Aura staff, who will make you feel welcome and comfortable. You can also join the group activities, such as the yoga classes, the karaoke nights, and the beach parties. Cox's Aura offers single rooms, free Wi-Fi, and 24/7 security for solo travelers.Read More
